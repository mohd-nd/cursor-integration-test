.NET 10 is the next major release of Microsoft's open-source, cross-platform development framework, expected to be released in November 2025. As a Standard Term Support (STS) release, .NET 10 continues the evolution of the unified .NET platform, bringing performance improvements, enhanced cloud-native capabilities, and new language features for C# 14. The release focuses on further optimizing runtime performance, improving developer productivity with better tooling, and expanding support for modern application development patterns including AI integration, containerization, and microservices architectures. .NET 10 builds upon the foundation established by previous versions, offering developers a robust and versatile platform for building web applications, APIs, desktop apps, mobile apps, games, and IoT solutions across Windows, Linux, and macOS.
